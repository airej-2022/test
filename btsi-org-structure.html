BTSI Org. Structure

BTSI has operationalized 4 strategic functions in order to realize its aspirations and achieve its targets

Key functions - Futuer Methods of Constractions (FMC)
Shape and lead the transformation of the construction industry by
  • Scouting and localizing advanced technology providers
  • Supporting local FMC innovation
  • Increasing FMC awareness
  • Providing a variety of services to FMC providers
Key functions - Upskill
Identify & build BT capabilities for sector workforce by
  • Identifying the key skills gaps in the BT sector
  • Creating standardized curricula and competency framework for BT
  • Delivering trainings and expert advisory to bridge the skills gaps
  • Operationalizing advisory services to support existing BT factories
  • Publishing BT related content and publications
Key functions - Supply
Stimulate MMC supply & increase its competitiveness by:
  • Scouting for new innovative & competitive MMC providers
  • Supporting existing providers to expand and modernize
  • Communicating with SIDF and funding the providers
  • Providing financial and non-financial services to MMC providers
Key functions - Demand
Drive MMC adoption in the Saudi construction market by
  • Match-making developers with MMC providers / contractors
  • Collaborating with Eskan program to increase the adoption of MMCs
  • Supporting in the MMC delivery of selected public sector projects
  • Setting up an MMC marketplace
