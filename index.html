Leading The Way In
Modern Constructions

Building Technology Stimulus Initiative was launched as a part of the housing eco-system, in order to lay the foundation of moving from traditional to modern construction, through investing in advanced technologies that contributes to coping with industrial advancement, emphasizing the Kingdom’s leadership role in this field and the efforts put into enabling citizens to obtain the house that matches their expectations of high quality and competitive cost in a shorter lead-time. To insure achieving the aspirations of the Kingdom and its citizens; the initiative is overseen by the Housing Program, the National Industrial Developments and Logistics Program, and supported by the Private Sector Stimulus Plan

Modern Methods
of Construction
Modern Methods of Construction (MMC) cover new and improved ways to perform the construction process. Using MMCs will lead to higher quality outputs and faster construction times, while decreasing the overall lifetime costs of the project. BTSI is working to improve the supply and demand of 6 MMC technologies in the Kingdom

Future Methods of
Construction

Future Methods of Construction (FMC) cover futuristic approaches to construction that have not yet been
adopted fully internationally and regionally. BTSI is looking to support FMC providers in the localization of
technology providers in the Kingdom.
